❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating stories

⏱️ How long does it take to create a story?

+
Stories are generated in just 20-30 seconds! Our AI works quickly to create a complete, personalized story with multiple pages and illustrations.

👶 Are the stories appropriate for all ages?

+
Yes! Our stories are designed for children ages 3-12. The content is always age-appropriate, positive, and includes valuable life lessons.

💾 Can I save and share stories?

+
Absolutely! You can save your favorite stories to read later, and view all your recently created stories in the Gallery section.

🎨 What themes are available?

+
We offer Adventure, Fantasy, Space, Ocean themes, plus a Custom option where you can describe your own unique theme! Each theme creates different types of magical stories.

🔐 Do I need to create an account?

+
No account needed! You can start creating stories right away. Your stories are automatically saved in the Gallery and Favorites sections.

About Kids Story

An AI-powered children's story generator built as a Final Year Project in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. We combine large language models, fine-tuned story generation, and AI image synthesis to create personalized picture books for kids aged 2–12.

🤖 The AI Behind the Stories

📖

Story Generation

Powered by Llama 3.3 70B via Groq API. Stories follow a real narrative arc — setup, wrong choice, consequence, realization, resolution — inspired by classic fables.

🎨

Image Generation

Each page gets a unique illustration using FLUX.1 image models. Scene prompts are extracted per page using a lightweight 8B model. Images are stored permanently via Cloudinary.

🔬

Fine-tuned Model

We fine-tuned GPT-2 on 50,000 children's stories from the TinyStories dataset. Our model achieved perplexity of 3.68 vs base GPT-2's 10.44 — a 2.8× improvement in children's story generation.
